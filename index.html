What happens if my collection day changes?+

Update your request with the new day. Once service is confirmed, schedule-change instructions will be included with your plan.

Do I need to be home?+

No. As long as the bins are accessible, service can be completed without anyone home.

Can you handle more than four bins?+

Absolutely. Choose “4+ bins” in the estimator and add the exact count in your booking notes for a custom quote.

Is this a long-term contract?+

No. Plans are designed to stay flexible. Final cancellation and pause details are included when service is confirmed.
